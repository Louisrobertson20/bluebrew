About Us

Ben and Jem are a couple from West Sussex who founded Blue Brew through their passion for coffee and desire to build their own business.

After travelling the globe and experiencing coffee in many different cultures, from walking through the coffee plantations in Vietnam to sitting back and sipping on flat whites in Melbourne’s bustling Degraves Street, the couple were keen to get started on setting up their own coffee business upon returning to the UK.

They fast learnt the power of coffee (along with good conversation!) in bringing people together and providing a comfort quite unlike any other.

Blue Brew is available for private hire, whatever the occasion, we would love to bring our delicious speciality coffee and homemade treats to you and your guests. Please get in touch!

Our Story



From then...

To now...



Our story began in early 2019 when we took an excited 2 hour drive to pick up our vintage horsebox trailer from Elstree Studios in London, where it had previously been used as a prop in a popular Netflix show!

It took the two of us around 5 months to undertake the mammoth task of converting our trailer from an empty (minus the manure!) shell to the fully kitted-out coffee box it is today.
